About the Campaign

Green Earth is an initiative that coordinates community tree planting, restores green cover, and supports local stewardship. We partner with volunteers and local groups to plant resilient species that support biodiversity and provide food and shade.

  • Restore habitats and support wildlife
  • Improve air quality and local microclimates
  • Engage communities in long-term stewardship
